Opening Plenary

The Opening Plenary will feature statements from the President of the General Assembly, the Secretary-General, the Chair of the Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities; an eminent person actively engaged in disability issues and a representative of non-governmental organizations in consultative status with the Economic and Social Council.

Speaking order as follows:

(1) H.E. Mr. John William Ashe, President of the General Assembly

(2) H.E. Mr. Ban Ki-moon, Secretary-General

(3) Ms. Maria Soledad Cisternas Reyes, Chair of the Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities

(4) Mr. Stevie Wonder, Award-winning songwriter and musician, United Nations Messenger of Peace

(5) Mr. Yannis Vardakastanis, President of the European Disability Forum

  • Sep 23 2013
  • 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM
  • General Assembly Hall (NLB)
