UNICEF Executive Board adopts United Nations Papersmart model for major meeting.

In response to the mandate of the General Assembly on Revitalization of the General Assembly and in line with the Secretary-General's objective of increasing efficiency and provision of e-services to Member States, the Meetings and Publishing Division of the Department for General Assembly and Conference Management (DGACM) through the Integrated Sustainable PaperSmart Services (ISPS) Secretariat, has developed an effective and innovative model, which provides an integrated re-engineered, lean approach to managing meetings and documentation through strategic utilization of information technology, to service delegates with the varied methods of accessing documentation, while making efficient use of our resources and by avoiding waste.
